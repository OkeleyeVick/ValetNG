Our Story
Our Story

How it all started

ValetNg was born out of a simple yet powerful idea: to make parking effortless and enjoyable. Our journey began when a group of parking enthusiasts realized that valet services could be more than just a practicality – they could be an experience.

Founded in the heart of Lagos by Mohammed-Ashraf Abubakar, ValetNg quickly gained recognition for its commitment to transforming parking into a seamless and elegant affair. Our name, ValetNg, embodies our dedication to providing 'Next-Generation' valet services.

We are not just a valet service; we are your partner in parking. Whether it's a glamorous event, a high-end restaurant, or a corporate function, we bring unmatched professionalism, advanced technology, and a touch of hospitality to every curbside interaction.

At ValetNg, we believe that convenience should be exceptional, and parking should be a pleasure, not a chore. We are on a relentless quest to redefine the valet parking experience, one satisfied customer at a time.

Join us on this journey and experience parking, the ValetNg way – where convenience meets luxury
